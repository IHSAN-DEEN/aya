In a world obsessed with speed, we have lost the art of stillness.

We build systems to manage data, but we have no system to manage our hearts for the sake of Allah.

aya is not just a CLI. It is a reminder. A digital companion for the Muslim's spiritual journey.

Use it to align your workflow with your purpose.

The Nuance (Ihsan)

We optimize our code, but what about our hearts? Aya turns your terminal into a spiritual companion.

The "Missing" Void

You're on a flight. No signal. Does your connection to Allah stop? No. We implemented Offline Prayers & Hijri Caching. Once fetched, you're set for the month.

Grace in Mistakes

You type al fatiha but the system expects Al-Fatiha. We added Fuzzy Matching because the effort counts. Allah is Merciful with our stumbling, so our tools should be too.

Respecting Differences

The Ummah is diverse. We added Madhab Selection (Hanafi/Standard) in aya init. We don't force a single view; we accommodate the richness of our tradition.

Continuity of Deeds

New laptop? Don't lose your Hifz progress. We added Export/Import commands. Your spiritual data is precious; it moves with you.
